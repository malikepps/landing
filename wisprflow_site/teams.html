Frequently asked questions

How do I upgrade to a Team or Enterprise account?

Option 1: Direct setup

Go to admin.wisprflow.ai and create an account using your work email address.

Option 2: Through the app

Create an account in the Flow app using your work email address

Navigate to Settings > Plans and Billing

You'll be redirected to the admin dashboard to complete setup

For step-by-step guidance, view a more detailed tutorial here

What if I already have an account?

To create or join a Flow Team, you must use your work email address. If you currently have a personal account with a different email, you'll need to create a new account with your work email.

Note: We don't currently support transferring your Dictionary or usage statistics between accounts, but this feature is on our development roadmap.
