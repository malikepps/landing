Why We Built Collective for Mission-Driven Organizations

Digital Marketing Control

Collective puts nonprofits back in control of their digital marketing. With our platform, your content ALWAYS reaches your entire community—no algorithms deciding who sees your message, no hidden feeds, no missed connections. Every update, every story, every call to action reaches every supporter in your contact list, whether or not they've joined your community on Collective.

Take control of your marketing →

Sustainable Growth

Collective makes sustainable growth possible through two powerful mechanisms. First, we make recurring giving natural—inspired by creator economy platforms like Patreon and Substack, supporters choose impact tiers that match their capacity, turning one-time donors into lasting partners. Second, we show your content to people in their neighborhoods who are likely to resonate with your mission, helping you build a local community that's deeply invested in your work.

Build sustainable growth →

Connection

Collective is a platform built for community, giving nonprofits the ability to truly connect with and "give back" to their donors. Beyond just accepting donations, you can share impact stories, celebrate milestones, and create meaningful relationships with supporters. Built-in chat offers a private space for deeper discussions, fostering authentic connection and conversation with contacts you already have. This is more than a donation platform—it's a space where your community thrives together.

Connect with your community →

Simplicity

We make it easy to run and set up your Collective space. We handle the original setup—including branding, describing your mission, and bringing in your content—so you can focus on what matters most: your work. We link with your CRM for seamless audience management and integrate with Instagram for automatic cross-posting, eliminating the need to juggle multiple platforms. Your only job is to invite your community and share your impact.
